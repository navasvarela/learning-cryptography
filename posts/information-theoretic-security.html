Abstract

This article introduces Information-Theoretic Security.

Introduction

A big number of cryptographic protocols and encryption schemes that are based on the hardness of specific mathematical problems. For example:

  • RSA, relies on the difficulty of solving the factoring problem
  • ElGamal, relies on the difficulty of solving the discrete logarithm problem
